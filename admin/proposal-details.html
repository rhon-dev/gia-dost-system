Portable seismic sensor for landslide-prone slopes

SubmittedR. Aquino · Regional Office · ₱ 760,000

Description & objectives

A ruggedized, battery-efficient seismic sensor node that detects micro-tremors and soil-movement signatures preceding landslides, transmitting alerts over LoRa to a barangay early-warning station.

Objectives: deploy 10 nodes across 2 high-risk slopes; validate detection lead-time against historical events; publish calibration methodology.
