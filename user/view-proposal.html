Solar-powered water purification for rural barangays

Endorsed Department of R&D · ₱ 480,000

Description & objectives

This project develops a low-cost, solar-powered water purification unit designed for off-grid rural barangays with limited access to potable water. Each unit combines photovoltaic panels with a multi-stage filtration and UV-disinfection system.

Objectives: (1) Build and field-test 5 prototype units across 3 pilot barangays; (2) Reduce water-borne illness incidence by a measurable margin; (3) Produce an open hardware spec for local replication.
